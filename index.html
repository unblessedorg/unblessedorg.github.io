The Sneeze Crisis

The Human Factor

With 8 billion humans each sneezing 2-3 times daily, over 20 billion human sneezes occur every day. The vast majority go unblessed, creating a massive blessing deficit.

The Animal Kingdom

Animals sneeze too! From elephants to house cats, countless animal sneezes have never received a single blessing throughout evolutionary history.

Our Mission

At Unblessed.org, we're raising awareness about this critical issue and empowering global citizens to become active blessing participants. Together, we can address this challenge if each person says just 43 bless-yous per day!
